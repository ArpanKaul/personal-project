DJing: An Aural Art
Turntabilism or Disc Jockeys were typically always the life of the party. From spinning records to controlling the rhythm of a party, DJ's in the 70's like DJ Kool Herc revolutionized breakbeats and laid the foundation for MC's to come about and actually 'spit' lyrics over a beat. Prior to lyricism and famous DJ's like Kool Herc, Grandmaster Flash and Jazzy Jeff, typically MCs would come out to warm up the party to introduce the DJ, but times have changed.
