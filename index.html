HIP-HOP IS KNOWLEDGE
It is an art movement. A lifestyle. 4 elements. It has origins ranging back to the 1970's in the Bronx, primarily led by African American and Latin youth. It was an underground momvement that arose from post-industrial and ravaged South Bronx.
It is a form of expression that has taken the world by storm and can be categorized into four different categories: Graffiti: Visual, B-boying: Physical, DJing: Aural, MCing: Oral and lastly the supreme over arching principle of KNOWLEDGE.
It is a rhytmic avante garde art which has incorperated styles from salsa, bonga drums, jazz, funk, blues and rock and roll. Some of the earliest proprietors included now famous DJ Kool Herc, DJ Afrika Bambaataa, Grandmaster Flash and the Furious Five.
